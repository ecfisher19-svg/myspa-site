How it works

From bare walls to dream spa in three steps

01
Scan your room

Open MySpa on your iPhone Pro and slowly pan around your bathroom. Our AR engine uses LiDAR to capture every wall, window, and surface with millimeter precision.

LiDAR · ARKit · RoomPlan
02
Design your spa

Your room appears in 3D — accurately dimensioned and ready to design. Drag and drop vanities, soaking tubs, walk-in showers, and fixtures until every inch is perfect.

3D Editor · Drag & Drop
03
Shop at real prices

Every fixture shows live pricing from Home Depot, Lowe's, and Wayfair. Add to your cart and check out — or share your design with your contractor and get to work.

Live Pricing · Instant Checkout
